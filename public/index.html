How does it work?

Singles Playing Doubles is a 4-week tennis program specially designed for singles!

📝 💬

Apply online

Give us some simple details to match you to the right group!

We just require that you're SINGLE, open to meet new people, live in Singapore, and can commit a few hours per week for 4 weeks.

No prior tennis experience needed!

👋 🤗

Group creation

Each "season", we'll create a group of 8 participants: 4 female + 4 male, and commit to maintaining this equal ratio!

Group members are manually screened and handpicked by us so that everyone can vibe (based on the details provided), wants to learn tennis (common interests!), and is in a similar age group.

And since we're explicit that this is tennis x dating, you can be rest assured that everyone's there for the same reasons ☺️

🎾 🏅

Learn Tennis!

Each week, we'll have a 2-hour session of tennis at a pre-booked court. Rackets, balls, and fitness drinks are provided -- just bring a towel!

Each session includes beginner-friendly, light-hearted coaching, designed to teach you the basics.

You'll alternate being on-court playing tennis, and off-court getting to know each other with fun conversation topics prepared by our team!

🏃 🍱

Surprise group activities

Each week, we'll also have a surprise group activity, so you get to spend even more time with the group! And, we top it all off with a nice group dinner. Yum!

Oh and back to tennis -- there may even be some competition and prizes up for grabs...

All of these are included in the program cost!

😍 🎉

Sparks flying?

Hopefully by the end of the program, you meet a special someone that you can continue to get to know better!

If not, all good -- you learned something new, and made some new friends ☺️
